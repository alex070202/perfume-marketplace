Shipping & Returns

Shipping

We offer free shipping on orders over 200 BGN in Bulgaria. Orders are processed within 1–2 business days and shipped via reliable couriers.

Estimated Delivery Times

  • Bulgaria: 1–3 business days
  • EU countries: 4–7 business days

Returns

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 14 days of delivery. The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and with the receipt.

How to Return

  1. Contact us at perfumeswap@gmail.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll provide return instructions

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.
